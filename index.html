Data Sources

Definitions

  • Yes: CSE explicitly mandated with policy guidance.
  • Partial: Exists but not branded CSE, or uneven.
  • No: No national integration.

Limitations

  • Law vs. practice gap exists in many countries.
  • Federal systems show sub-national variation.
  • Static snapshot — reflects May 2026 state.
